About Us

[About Us]

We help for growing your business

Indigrow Foods & Beverages Private Limited is an exporter and supplier entity that has been catering to small and massive product demands of the clients across the globe. We deal in a range of Indian Rice - Basmati Rice, Non Basmati Rice, and Idli Rice. We also deal in Kitchen Utensils like Stainless Steel, Copper, and Brass. For buying Fresh Fruits like Fresh Apple, Fresh Grapes, Fresh Pomegranate, and Fresh Banana, clients can count on us. We are a quality conscious firm, we thus deploy different quality measures to ensure that the offered products comply with the codes of the set quality norms.

Indigrow Foods & Beverages Private Limited is a sole proprietorship firm, operating under the able guidance of Mr. Dheeraj & Mr. Vijay Kakkar (Owner). Working under their leadership, we have cemented our strong position amid the leading industry players. We are based in Karnal (Haryana, India) and as a company, we started our company operations in 2019.

The Facility

Indigrow Foods & Beverages Private Limited has invested a large sum for establishing its own facility to process the varied demands of the clients in a convenient manner. We procure products from the trusted names, store under hygiene conditions and process further as per the demands.
